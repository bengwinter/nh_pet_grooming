OUR PHILOSOPHY

With over 20 years of grooming experience our goal is to help educate our clients on the proper care of their cherished pets. This enables them to spot problems and potential health issues.

We strive to get to know you and your pet so that we can accommodate any special needs and provide that level of unique attention that both you and your pet deserve.

All animals are treated with kindness and respect. Our policy puts all dogs’ health and comfort above cosmetics and aesthetics. If we deem the coat is too matted, the only humane thing to do is a short clip. We will NEVER hurt an animal to save the coat.
